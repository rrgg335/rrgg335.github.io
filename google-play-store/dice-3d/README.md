# Dice3D

Dice3D is a simple and lightweight 3D dice rolling app available on Google Play.

The app is designed for quick, fair, and visually realistic dice rolls, with a clean interface and no user accounts required.

---

## 📱 Live on Google Play

You can download Dice3D directly from the Google Play Store:

[https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.coolfolks.mrdice](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.coolfolks.mrdice)
