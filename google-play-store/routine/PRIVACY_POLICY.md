# Privacy Policy for Routine

**Last updated:** 17 January 2026

Routine ("the app") is developed and published by **Rohan G** ("the developer").

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Routine application available on Google Play.

## Information We Collect

Routine does **not** collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses, or precise location data.

All routines, tasks, and preferences created in the app are stored **locally on the user’s device**.

## Third-Party Advertising

Routine uses third-party advertising services, including **Google AdMob**, to display advertisements within the app.

These third-party services may automatically collect and process certain information, including:
- Device or other identifiers, including the Google Advertising ID (AAID)
- Ad interaction data (such as ad impressions and clicks)
- App diagnostics and crash-related information

This information is used for advertising delivery, ad performance measurement, analytics, and fraud prevention.

## User Choices

Users can limit or reset the Google Advertising ID through their device settings. This allows users to control how ads are personalized.

## App Functionality and Permissions

The app may use the following features:
- **Notifications:** Used only to remind users about their routines and tasks.
- **Internet access:** Required to display advertisements and for basic app functionality.

Routine itself does not transmit or store personal data on external servers operated by the developer.

## App Information and Performance

Basic diagnostic information such as crash logs may be collected automatically by Google Play services and third-party SDKs to:
- Monitor app stability
- Diagnose errors and crashes
- Improve overall app performance

This information does not include personally identifiable data.

## Data Sharing

Some data collected through third-party SDKs may be shared with advertising and analytics partners solely for the purposes of advertising, analytics, and maintaining app functionality.

The developer does **not** sell user data.

## Security

Data transmitted by third-party advertising services is encrypted in transit using industry-standard security practices.

## Children’s Privacy

Routine is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer so appropriate action can be taken.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
