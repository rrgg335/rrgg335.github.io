# Privacy Policy for Minux

**Last updated:** 10 May 2026

Minux ("the app") is developed and published by **Rohan Garg (rrgg335)** ("the developer").

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and stored when you use the Minux application available on Google Play.

## Information We Collect

Minux does **not** collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses, or precise location data.

The app does not require user accounts or user registration.

Wallpaper settings and preferences are stored **locally on the user’s device**.

## Third-Party Advertising

Minux uses third-party advertising services, including **Google AdMob**, to display advertisements within the app.

These third-party services may collect and process certain data automatically, including:
- Device or other identifiers, including the Google Advertising ID (AAID)
- Ad interaction data (such as ad impressions and clicks)
- App diagnostics and crash-related information

This data is used for advertising delivery, ad performance measurement, analytics, and fraud prevention.

## User Choices

Users can limit or reset the Google Advertising ID through their device settings. This allows users to control how ads are personalized.

## Data Sharing

Some data collected through third-party SDKs may be shared with advertising and analytics partners solely for the purposes of advertising, analytics, and maintaining app functionality.

The developer does not sell user data.

## App Functionality and Permissions

The app may use the following features:

- **Wallpaper services:** Required to apply and display live wallpapers on the device
- **Internet access:** Required to display advertisements and for basic app functionality.

Minux does not transmit or store personal data on external servers operated by the developer.

## App Information and Performance

Basic diagnostic information such as crash logs may be collected automatically by Android system services or Google Play services to:

- Monitor app stability
- Diagnose errors and crashes
- Improve overall app performance

This information does not include personally identifiable data.

## Data Sharing

Minux does not sell, share, or transmit personal user data to third parties.

## Security

Any system-level data processed by Android or Google Play services is handled using industry-standard security practices.

## Children’s Privacy

Minux is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer so appropriate action can be taken.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
