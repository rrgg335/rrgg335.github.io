# Minux

Minux is a live wallpaper app featuring animated wallpapers for Android devices.

The app focuses on lightweight animated backgrounds designed to give your home screen a clean and dynamic look.

---

## 📱 Live on Google Play

You can download Minux directly from the Google Play Store:

[Get on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.rohangarg.minux)
